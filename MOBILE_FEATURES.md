## 🚀 How Users Install as App:

### iPhone/iPad:
1. Open site in Safari
2. Tap Share button (↗️)
3. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap "Add"

### Android:
1. Open site in Chrome
2. Tap menu (⋮)
3. Tap "Install app" or "Add to Home screen"
4. Tap "Install"
